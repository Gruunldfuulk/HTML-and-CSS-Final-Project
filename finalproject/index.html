A small histoy of Synthwave

Synthwave originates from the mid 2000s. French acts including David Grellier (College),
Kavinsky, and Justice are recognized as the pioneers contributing to the early synthwave sound.
The synthwave sound got big jumps in popularity from the movice Drive(2011), the Hotline Miami games
and the nextflix show Stranger Things. Synthwave leans heavy on scifi, 80's, cyberpunk, and horror
for not only it's album art and sound, but it's track titles.
